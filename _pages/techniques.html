Universal Baked Pasta Rules

The Golden Rules

  • 1. Undercook pasta by 2 minutes—it finishes in the oven
  • 2. Save pasta water—starchy water binds sauces
  • 3. Never add oil to pasta water—sauce won't adhere
  • 4. Layer strategically—sauce on bottom prevents sticking
  • 5. Rest before cutting—8-10 minutes minimum

Make-Ahead Tips

  • • All baked pastas refrigerate 3-5 days
  • • Freeze unbaked for best results
  • • Add extra sauce when reheating
  • • Oven reheating beats microwave
  • • Many dishes taste better the next day!
